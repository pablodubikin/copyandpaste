About
Hi! My name is Pablo, and I'm a software developer. I created this app to go along with my CV.

It's simple: just paste some text into the text-area, share the page's url with someone, and they get your pasted text.

Editing is open: anyone who can watch the contents, can edit the contents. And they get updated with everyone watching, live!

The app simply serves as my showcase app, and is not intended for let's say production usage.

Please, refer to the the github repository for more info about the app, or check my linkedin.
